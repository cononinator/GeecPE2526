EPC23101 Behavioral Model Notes

Instructions: include EPC23101_model.net and call subcircuit EPC23101.

If you wish to use EPC2302 or another EPC power FET in a simulation alongside EPC23101, then 
its model must also be included. These models are provided in EPCGaNLibrary.lib. The desired FET 
must be linked to a symbol in the schematic.

An example 48V-12V buck converter testbench is provided, which uses both EPC23101 and EPC2302, see
EPC23101_sim.asc.

The LTSPICE model EPC23101_model.net is a simplified version of the IC that simulates the device's
functionality and approximate electrical performance (e.g., rise/fall times, power dissipation).
In the case of a discrepancy between this behavioral model and the product datasheet, refer to the
datasheet. Please report any model issues or convergence errors to AskaGaNExpert@epc-co.com or your
field application engineer.
